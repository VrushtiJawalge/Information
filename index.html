Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj

Known for his valor, leadership, and strategic acumen, Shivaji Maharaj challenged the dominance of the Mughal Empire and other regional powers.

Born at the Shivneri Fort in present-day Maharashtra, he was deeply inspired by his mother Jijabai and his guru Dadoji Konddev. He took the oath of Swarajya (self-rule) and gradually carved out a powerful kingdom through both diplomacy and military skill. One of his greatest achievements was his coronation as Chhatrapati (monarch) in 1674 at Raigad Fort
